Mission

At Fallbrook Research we're leveraging data science, quantitative finance, and operational analytics. Assets are flowing out of discretionary and into quantitative systematic strategies which have produced far more consistent alpha. We seek to leverage operational analytics for systematic investment and algorithmic trading strategies. Building on statistical and machine learning methods supported by big data with quantitative finance applications across the entire spectrum of the investment workflows; alpha, beta, risk management, and execution & trade execution.

One of the most important developments in investing centers on the shift to systematic and rules-based investment strategies as compared with purely passive (index investing) to semi-passive (smart beta) to quant (factor-based) and other investment strategies. Processes evolution can help structure teams to become more quantitative and systematic. Perform iterative data analysis at scale.

Factors like momentum and value work but they don't work all the time. We move beyond traditional linear regression algorithms used by many quants to better fit strategies. At Fallbrook we leverage machine learning techniques to identify strategy fit within the current context and improve visiblity to shifts in risk and performance attribution.
